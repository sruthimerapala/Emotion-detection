# Religious texts
**Religious texts are texts related to a religious tradition.**
They differ from literary texts by being a compilation or discussion of
* beliefs
* mythologies
* commandments or laws
* ritual practices
The relative authority of religious texts develops over time and is derived from the the ratification, enforcement, and its use across generations.
Some religious texts are accepted or categorized as
1. canonical
2. non canonical
